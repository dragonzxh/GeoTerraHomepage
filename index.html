Methodology & Technology

The Power Mix Head

An intelligent and powerful mixing head designed to inject stabilizing agents directly into soft soils (muck, clay, swampy ground). It mixes the soil on the spot to form a semi-rigid strong layer.

RPM Speed

50 - 120 RPM High Power Mixing

Productivity

1,000 m³ / day / set up

Intelligent Control System

Zhongneng has independently developed a set of intelligent, digitalized, and highly efficient equipment. The system features automated dosing, scientific quantification, and "One-button stop" management.

Stabilizing Agents

Cement, Fly Ash, GGBS & Special Additives

Treatment Depth

Standard operation: 0 ~ 5 meters

Why Choose In-Situ Solidification?

Eco-Friendly

No excavation and removal of unsuitable soil. Prevents pollution and saves energy by eliminating disposal transportation.

High Efficiency

No surcharge preloading. No long settlement waiting period. Hardened crust forms the next day.

Cost Saving

Save costs on excavation, sand replacement, and disposal fees. Reduces construction timeline significantly.

Verified Quality & Time Savings

We use rigorous testing (SCPT, DCPT, Plate Bearing, CBR) to ensure soil strength.

Laboratory & Field Testing

Our treated soil achieves high bearing capacity verified by lab reports and field tests.

  • Soaked CBR: > 25% (Example: 26.8%)
  • Bearing Capacity: Sufficient for heavy machinery movement the next day.
  • Curing Period: Max 28 days for full strength.
