Soft Ground Challenges in Malaysia

Soft ground such as clay and silt are usually found in abundance in the coastal plains of Peninsular and East Malaysia.

Characteristics

  • Low shear strength (< 40kPa)
  • High natural moisture content
  • Highly compressible & low permeability

Main Geotechnical Problems

Low bearing capacity, unpredictable settlement, and stability issues.

Methodology & Technology

An intelligent mixing head designed to inject stabilizing agents directly into soft soils (muck, clay, swamp) to form a semi-rigid strong layer.
