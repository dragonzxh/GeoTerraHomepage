Company Profile

Geoterra Solutions Sdn Bhd specializes in advanced soil stabilization. Using in-situ shallow soil mix and solidification technology, we transform soft ground into strong and stable foundations.


In collaboration with Zhongneng Green Bay (Zhejiang), we bring cutting-edge green engineering from China to Malaysia, providing a new dimension in geotechnical treatment that is superior to conventional methods.

Shallow In-situ Soil-mix Solidification

An intelligent geotechnical method applying stabilizing agents directly to soft soils to form a hard semi-rigid crust.

Intelligent Equipment

Proprietary "Power Mix Head" with 50-120 RPM and twin-hose detachable mixing. Integrated with a smart dosing control console for scientific quantification.

Custom Stabilizing Agents

Composite mineral-based cementitious materials (Cement, GGBS, Gypsum) mixed with special additives to turn hydrophilic soil into hydrophobic foundations.

High Performance

  • Treatment Depth: 0 ~ 5 meters
  • Productivity: 1,000 m続 / day / set
  • High Bearing Capacity (CBR > 25%)
